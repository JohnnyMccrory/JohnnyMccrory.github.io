A Step by Step Guide for Scanning QR Code on Android Phone

1. Enabling Camera Permission#

Scanning QR codes is commonplace in the modern world. It can be a fast and easy way to open up a link, join a WiFi network or to share contact information. However, you may not know how to quickly set up camera permissions on your Android device to make sure you can scan QR codes. Here’s a quick guide on enabling camera permissions:

First, open your device’s settings and select Apps. Then, click on the specific application that you are looking to grant camera permission. Click on the Permissions tab, and then select Camera. Toggle the camera switch on for this application.

If the app is a system app, you will need to open the Settings and select Application Manager. Search for the system app and select Permissions. Toggle the camera switch on for this application.

Once you have granted camera permission, you will be able to scan any QR code effortlessly. You can do so by opening the app, allowing it to access the camera and then shifting the QR code into the camera viewfinder. For latest devices, a QR code scanner is built-in directly into the camera app.

Following these simple steps will enable you to quickly scan QR codes using your Android device. Remember to enable camera permission for each application individually. Enjoy the convenience of quick QR code scanning!

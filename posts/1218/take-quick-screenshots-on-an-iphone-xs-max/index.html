Q1: How can I take a quick screenshot on my iPhone XS Max? A1: To take a quick screenshot on your iPhone XS Max, press and hold the side button on the right side plus either of the volume buttons. Then release both buttons and you will see a preview of the screen capture.

Q2: What do I do if I want to edit the screenshot I just took? A2: After you take a screenshot, you can tap the screenshot preview at the bottom left of the screen to bring up the edit menu. From here you can crop, rotate, add markup, and choose a color palette.

Q3: What happens if I take a long screenshot? A3: If you take a long screenshot, it will be saved as a multi-page PDF document. You’ll find the PDF document in the Photos app.

Q4: How do I share my screenshots? A4: After you take a screenshot, you can tap and hold the screenshot preview at the bottom left of the screen to bring up a menu. From there you can share your screenshot via Messages, Mail, or social media apps.

Q5: What if I want to delete screenshots I’ve taken? A5: You can delete your screenshots from the Photos app. Tap on the screenshot that you want to delete, then tap the trash can icon at the bottom right of the screen. You can also delete multiple screenshots at once by tapping the “Select” option at the top right, then tapping the trash can icon for each screenshot you want to delete.

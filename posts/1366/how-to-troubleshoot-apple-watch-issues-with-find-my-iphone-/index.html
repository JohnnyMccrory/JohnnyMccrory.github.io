Q2: How does Find My iPhone work on the Apple Watch?

A3: To troubleshoot your Apple Watch, open the Find My iPhone app on your iPhone and select your Apple Watch. If the Apple Watch is online, you can force restart it or erase all of its content and settings. If the Apple Watch is offline, you can put it in Lost Mode or remotely erase it.

A4: Lost Mode is a feature of Find My iPhone that lets you lock your Apple Watch with a passcode and display a message so that whoever finds it can contact you. You can also track the device’s location and play a sound if it’s nearby.

Q5: How do I turn off Lost Mode?

A5: To turn off Lost Mode, open Find My iPhone on your iPhone and select your Apple Watch. Then, click the “Turn Off Lost Mode” button. You can also turn off Lost Mode by entering the passcode on your Apple Watch.

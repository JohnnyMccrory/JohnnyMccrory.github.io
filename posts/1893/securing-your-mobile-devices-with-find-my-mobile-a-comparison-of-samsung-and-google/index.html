Additionally, users can set up a lock screen on their device to keep their data secure and can erase their data if necessary. The service also offers additional features, such as remote device tracking, app lock, and contact backup.

Google’s Find My Device service is available on Android devices, and is already activated on devices that have signed into their Google account.

1. Overview of Barcode Scanning#

Barcode scanning is a fast and efficient way of collecting data using handheld devices. With your Android phone, you can scan barcodes on a variety of items including books, products, and even medicines. This article will provide an overview of how to scan barcodes with an Android phone.

When you’re ready, press the shutter button to capture the barcode. Your Android phone will automatically decode and read the barcode and display the information within a few seconds.

Barcode scanning allows you to quickly obtain information about a product without manual entry. This can be useful when you are purchasing items from a store, as you can quickly check if the barcode is valid or not. It is also useful for medical professionals who need to scan medicines to ensure that they are using the right drugs.

To ensure accurate barcode scanning, you should make sure that your phone is held steady while taking the picture. You should also make sure that the barcode is visible in the viewfinder of your phone’s camera. Finally, you should make sure that the barcode is kept clean and free from scratches as this will affect the accuracy of the scanning process.

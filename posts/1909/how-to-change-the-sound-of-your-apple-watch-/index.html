1. How to turn on sound on Apple Watch#

The Apple Watch is a great device that allows you to do many things. But one of the less intuitive features is how to turn on and change the sound of your Apple Watch. Fortunately, it is a very simple task that requires only a few steps. Here’s how you can do it.

First, you need to open the Settings app on your Apple Watch. To do this, go to the Home screen and tap the Settings icon. Once you have opened the Settings app, you need to scroll down and tap on Sounds & Haptics.

Next, you will see a switch labeled Sound. Tap it to turn the sound on. You can also adjust the volume of the sound by using the volume slider directly below the switch.

Finally, you can choose a sound for your Apple Watch. To do this, scroll down until you find Alert Tone and then tap on it. You will then be presented with a list of different sounds and haptic feedback to choose from.

That’s it! You have now successfully changed the sound and volume of your Apple Watch.

Next, you can also turn off the sound on your Apple Watch by using Airplane Mode. Go to the Settings app on your watch and go to the ‘Airplane Mode’ option. Toggle the Airplane Mode option to ‘On’ to turn off the sound on your watch.

Lastly, you can also turn off the sound on your watch by going to the Notification Center. Go to the Settings app on your watch and go to the ‘Notification Center’ option. Then toggle the ‘Do Not Disturb’ option to ‘On’ to turn off all sounds except alarms.
